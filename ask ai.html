How Our AI Can Help You

PharmAssist AI is designed to support your healthcare journey with personalized assistance:

  • Provide medication information and dosage guidance
  • Answer questions about drug interactions and side effects
  • Send medication reminders and refill notifications
  • Help locate nearby pharmacies and check medicine availability
  • Assist with understanding prescription instructions
  • Provide general health information and wellness tips
  • Connect you with a human pharmacist when needed

Note: PharmAssist AI is for informational purposes only and does not replace professional medical advice. Always consult with your healthcare provider for medical decisions.
